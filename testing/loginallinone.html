Volunteer Board



Many community groups organize service projects such as cleanup, donations, painting, and  landscaping, but a number of these groups don’t have coordinating software to manage these projects. To avoid the hassel of sending emails and making calls, this is a solution available for any group  to use and offers public visibility of a volunteer project to spread their influence. You can now post projects  and details or view projects to select one to attend.
